**Loggning**

**NUC+Vector:** The car is equipped with Vector and NUC that automatically uploads logs when standing on Volvo Wi-Fi. Therefore, you do not need to take any trigger. However, you need to be very precise of the timing when issue occurred.

**DHU-bug reports**: Long press of play module Do not enter any menu after triggering the bug report. Wait until the bug report is ready and upload it.

**Climate logs:** Press sync temperature button in CSD 10 times.

**ADAS Hostlog:** for issues related to Pilot Assist, RSI, LKA, Curve Speed Assist. Make sure to also add a location (Pin on map, koordinates, roadname etc)
